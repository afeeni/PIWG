It's Personal

“It’s Personal”

The Word of God is Supreme PRIORITY! Absolutely nothing comes before it - it processes you into your best life (Psalms 84:11). It eradicates your past and fine-tune your future. It’s the most powerful source in the universe; and once received by studying your focus changes; you can now see a glimpse of your purpose that you will fulfil.


It gives life and inspiration to your spirit-life to propel your way; but it must be fueled with the Word to grow and live out its purpose once revealed.


The Word of God comes before family, friends, activities, and yes your workplace. Why, because it takes the Holy Word to lead you in your thoughts and your actions to live out a Christian Life. God wants full control and by obeying His word you give Him that.


The Word pulls down and discloses unnecessary thoughts and behavior. The effect is amazing; it’s an inner-outer body – euphoria impact that cannot be explained only experienced. You personally witness the miracle of a converted soul – your own. The Word is the glue that strengthens our relationship with Jesus Christ. There is no knowing Him without it; The Word!


Romans 15:4

For everything that was written in the past was written for our instruction, so that through endurance and the encouragement of the Scriptures we might have hope. Amen


The Word of God filters through the cortex of our brain and it reaches directly into places that have been infused by the world and it destroys the negative strongholds of our thinking …. (Cortex is the largest part of the human brain associated with higher brain functions such as thought and action…)


A negative stronghold penetrates our core values and it distorts our living and it hold us in situations, turmoil, confusion, depression, and unhealthy relationships longer than we should be. Strongholds, also keep us from coming out of sin and it pushes thoughts like; God will never forgive me, and I can’t live this Christian life. It keeps fear your primary focus and makes doubt your comfort zone; all mental thoughts that can be changed.


Stronghold – 2 Corinthians 10:5 Casting down imaginations and every high thing that exalts itself against the knowledge of God, and bringing into captivity every thought to the obedience of Christ.


All those negative thoughts you just read can be cast down and made to submit to the Word of God….


Jeremiah 29:11-13 (Its Personal)
