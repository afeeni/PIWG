CONVERTING A SOUL

Why is there such a fight? Because of what’s at stake – because it leads to converting a soul.

Conversion in the Biblical sense means turning a sinner to God (Acts 15:3) or more specifically “when, by the influence of divine grace in their souls, their whole life is changed, old things pass away, and all things become new (Acts 26:18). (Excerpts from www.biblestudytools.com/dictionaryconversion). To convert also means to change, reform or restructure – it indicates moving in a different direction.

Every Believer has a testimony – his or her own story of being brought from darkness to Light. Every Believer also has the POWER to convert. Power is authority, ability, expertise and/or influence. Galatians 5:19-21

We have been given this authority because Jesus Christ believes in us and lives in us. We have expertise and influence because we have the testimony of our own conversion of what God has done and is presently doing in our lives.

It is important that we understand the actuality and the reality of the magnitude of the AUTHORITY we have in our hands to convert a Soul. What strength we have to believe and to have the faith to pray with a soul - that they may live and not die!Listen carefully: I have given you authority [that you now possess] to tread on serpents and scorpions, and [the ability to exercise authority] over all the power of the enemy (Satan); and nothing will [in any way] harm you. Nevertheless do not rejoice at this, that the spirits are subject to you, but rejoice that your names are recorded in heaven.” Luke 10:19 (Amp.)

How has your life been impacted by a relationship with Jesus Christ?

Can you use your testimony as a tool to witness to gain a soul for Jesus Christ?

There is a Plan for every Converted Soul to be used by God. To open their eyes, and to turn them from darkness to light, and from the power of Satan unto God, that they may receive forgiveness of sins, and inheritance among them which are sanctified by faith that is in me. We must submit ourselves to God’s plan. “Thy will be done on earth as it is in heaven” (Matthew 6:10); “Therefore go and make disciples of all Nations baptizing them in the name of the Father, and of the Son, and the Holy Spirit (Matthew 28:18-19). .

We cannot let religion keep us from evangelizing a soul. A soul is a soul is a soul . . . and as long has it has breath, it can be converted!

Religion – is a doctrine, a policy and a set of guidelines, as well as it being an opinion that can be believable to live by and have faith in.Our purpose is to be educated in what we believe to break down or defame doctrines, policies and that keep others from the WORD of GOD! Man of God and Woman of God on the day of your Salvation accepting Jesus Christ as your Personal Savior you took on the duties and the responsibilities of the work of the Lord.

John 14:12 Verily, verily, I say unto you, He that believeth on me, the works that I do shall he do also; and greater works than these shall he do; because I go unto my Father. Along with Faith the size of a Mustard Seed you were also given courage, Commitment, hope, determination, creativity, trust and Faith to Gain Souls for Christ. You cannot have one without the other, Faith/Salvation without works are dead they work together, Salvation and Evangelizing.

Every Converted Soul Escapes Eternity in Hell

Proverbs 11:30 The fruit of the righteous is a tree of life; and he that winneth souls is wise.
On your day of Salvation instantaneously you were assigned to souls to reach out to. The Lord knew you were coming to Him and that you would have faith and grow. So simultaneously your assignment was prepared for you…for such a time as this.

Hell a place regarded in various religions as a spiritual realm of evil and suffering, often traditionally depicted as a place of perpetual fire beneath the earth where the wicked are punished after death. Converting a soul releases a life from the hand of the evil one and from the pit of Hell. How awesome it is to save someone from going to Hell!

And the devil that deceived them was cast into the lake of fire and brimstone, where the beast and the false prophet [are], and shall be tormented day and night forever and ever. Revelation 20:10

If we believe the Words of Bible that say that salvation has changed us, then we must believe the Words of the Bible that Hell exists. There is a hell and people do go there – but we have the authority to save a soul from the pit of hell.

Christ knows who His Children are and it is up to us to avail our lives to God’s purpose. Let’s love what Christ loves - His People. How can we say we love the Lord and not do His will? How can we take blessings after blessings and never look back to say “Lord, here am I Lord send me”?
