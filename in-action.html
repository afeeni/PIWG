PRAYER IN ACTION

Prayer is not just uttering words to our Savior – they are words waiting to happen to come alive in our lives.

Prayer must include a plan of action to propel you forward in a direction that has been revealed to you through prayer. In other words, you must do something to unlock the door that stands between the prayer request and the reality (manifestation) of that answered prayer to YOU!

Prayer spells ACTION!

Ask, and it shall be given you; seek, and ye shall find; knock, and it shall be opened unto you - Matthew 7:7 (KJV)





This is where we see the scripture come alive to us
  • Praying for a job – you then look for one
  • Praying for a job – you then look for one
  • Praying for purpose – do something volunteer (one action causes a re-action to something)
  • Praying for God to use you to help others - get involved in your church or community, take the initiative at work with an idea

Prayer shapes your life and applying Matthew 7:7 to your prayer life helps you to grow forward in Jesus Christ. Remember, when we pray God does hear us and He answers which . The prayers of the righteous AVAILETH MUCH! James 5:16

Also remember that Praying for others, that’s INTERCESSION interceding for someone else…these prayers are powerful…but, you cannot act on behalf of someone else they must act on their own..
