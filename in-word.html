In The Word

In The Word

After Salvation studying God’s Word is our Salvation. It’s the source of our survival and the guided post for our daily living; and the shaping of our destiny. You cannot have Salvation or a personal relationship with Jesus Christ without the Word of God. It is the Word of God that you have read, heard and believe that brings you to repentance..

It is where you find:

  • What God thinks of you and what He believes that you can accomplish in His Name Philippians 4:13
  • Your Will to Serve Him Joshua 22:5
  • Forgiveness Luke 6:37
  • Inspiration to never give up Philippians 3:14
  • A love that cannot be compared to any (John 15:13) you learn how to love and be loved
  • Motivation Proverbs 3:5 - 6
  • Peace Colossians 3:15
  • Revelation Ephesians 1:17
  • Inspiration 2 Timothy 3:16
  • Discernment Psalm 119:125
  • Wisdom how to live James 1: 5
  • Vision and Creativity Daniel 7:1
  • Hope Proverbs 13:12
  • Liberty 1 Corinthians 9:19
  • Unspeakable Joy 2 Chronicles 29:36
  • Understanding James 3:17 - James 1:5
  • Pushed to do more, be more and try again Philippians 3:14
  • Learning about Jesus is inspiring Romans 1:17 - 2 Corinthians 3:9
  • The Voice of the Lord Deuteronomy 13:4
  • Comfort 2 Thessalonians 2:17
  • How you can obey Him Deuteronomy 27:10
  • That you are forgiven over and over and over Hebrews 10:18
  • Your Faith is increased and inspired Hebrews 11:1
  • He loves you no matter what (He has that - No Matter what kind of love for you) Jeremiah 31:3
  • Truth for all things concerning you! Psalms 51:6
  • You find love, joy, hope, and encouragement within the pages of your bible… Proverbs 20:28

Jeremiah 29:13 And ye shall seek me, and find me, when ye shall search for me with all your heart.

There is nothing more splendor, magnificent, glorious or gratifying or even enchanting than to seek Christ in His Word…His presence transcends beyond normality and leaves a spectacular awe that makes you want so much more ...it soothes the Soul; as it allows a Soul Experience that cannot be explained Only Experienced –it’s like experiencing the joy of salvation over and over and over and over and over again: it’s mesmerizing….

Psalm 34:8 Oh taste and see that the Lord is good: blessed is the man that trusteth in him.

God Moments- sporadically drop based on the Word in You; that will cause a praise at anytime or the excitement of Joy will come and overtake you for a moment...because theWord in you gives God Something to Work with ...
