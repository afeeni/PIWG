A – Are we finished?
Z – No you have just begun….
A – I feel strange, what is that? Is it because I Believe?
Z – Your Overflow
A – What is overflow?
Z – It’s unspeakable Joy, that you can’t explain, but you know something is happening….
A – I can’t stop smiling
Z & A – Hugging
A – Will I always feel like this?
Z – No
A – Will I ever feel like this again?
Z - Yes
A – Why do I feel like I want more?
Z – That’s a good thing – it means you will go after it…to get more of this moment
A – I feel like screaming Jesus
Z – JESUS, JESUS, JESUS GLORY – Hallelujah
A & Z – JESUS GLORY, GLORY - Tears of JOY are flowing
Z – What if I don’t have any answers for you, I’m not a therapist or a counselor
A – I know but when you shared with my friend that God loved her she lit up like a light….
Z – That’s because she believed that He does love her. I think she just needed to be reminded..
Z – Do you know if your friend knows God?
A – I’m not sure, but I think so
Z – I told you also that God loves you. Do you believe that He loves you?
A – Not sure,
Z – We have been talking for a while do you think that I care about you?
A – Yes
Z – That’s the love of God in me-to love people I don’t know; it comes with Salvation
A – Explain Salvation to me
Z – Do you want to pray now for Salvation?
A – No, I want you to explain it to me first ….
Z – How do I explain to you what has changed my entire life, given me hope and have freed me from all my doubts and fears. How do I explain to you what has ripped out the old way of thinking and replaced it with something new … prayer, love, joy, and peace. How I can now say, not my will thy will be done…How I can now look and see a future prepared just for me. How now I can pray about a thing/situation/circumstances/ups and downs and know without a doubt that my prayers will be answered and that everything is and shall work out for MY Good. And as I am reading the Word it ministers to my broken places, my hurt places, and it heals areas of my life that I don’t even want to confront sometimes… Salvation has given a NEW Life.. A life that I could have never found on my own. A path only that could found in Salvation…. I now know why I was created, why God bought me into the world….and why I had to go through what I went through …
A – Can we pray now?
Z – But, I have not explained Salvation to you
A – Yes, you have….I can feel what you are saying about God, I can feel the Love and assurance that you have about the God’s Word and your life…
Z – Peace have entered between us…
Z – Father in Jesus name A is interested in knowing you she/he would like for you to accept her/him and bless her/him with Salvation
Z – A, I am getting ready to pray for you, your life is getting ready to change mentally, physically, and will be introduced to spirituality ….
Z – I’m going to lead you into prayer an as I pray repeat after me okay..
A – Okay.
Z – Father we come together in Jesus Name approaching your throne of Grace and Mercy, asking that you for give me of my sins. I know you died on the cross for me and I know all that you have done you have done it for me. Please come into my life, forgive me of my sins and have your way. In Jesus Name I pray Amen.
She’s Still bowed down –
Z – I’m wondering if she’s waiting to hear or feel God….
