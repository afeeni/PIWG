SALVATION

Salvation

When we are born, we come into a world of sin. "Behold, I was shapen in iniquity; and in sin did my mother conceive me. But I was born a sinner, yes, from the moment my mother conceived me. - Psalm 51:5

We come knowing how to do wrong - and we must be taught how to do right. Christ died on the cross because He knew we could never come to the place of righteousness on our own. For God so loved the world that he gave his only begotten Son that whosoever believeth in him should not perish, but have everlasting life. - John 3:16

We were born to be saved and given eternal life with the Father - this kind of love is unexplainable.We can know this kind of love - of Christ dying on the cross to save us and give us eternal life - by accepting Jesus Christ as our PERSONAL SAVIOR, the only One who can save us from going to hell. Without SALVATION, we go to hell as punishment for the wrong we continued to do since we were born. We are made right with God by placing our faith in Jesus Christ. And this is true for everyone who believes, no matter who we are.

All have sinned and have fallen short of the glory of God

For the wages of sin is death, but the gift of God is eternal life in Jesus Christ our Lord.

Prayer of Salvation

The most important step you will ever make in your life is when you pray this prayer – the Prayer of Salvation.

(Pray this prayer)
Father God in Heaven, I humbly come to you in Jesus’ Name to acknowledge and confess that I am a sinner and I'm asking for your forgiveness.

Your Word says in Romans 10:9-10: If you confess with your mouth, "Jesus is Lord," and believe in your heart that God raised him from the dead, you will be saved. For it is with your heart that you believe and are justified, and it is with your mouth that you confess and are saved.

I now know that you died on the cross for me and for the sins that I have done. Thank you for drawing me to your Son Jesus Christ and for saving my soul.
