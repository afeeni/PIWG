The Lens of Salvation

A world with a portal view from God’s perspective. A world God has formed in-lined with (thy will be done on earth as it is in heaven) from Genesis to Revelation; which is the embodiment/personification of Jesus Christ.


To be in the world but not of the world - to attempt to live life with God’s understanding


Isaiah 55:8 For my thoughts are not your thoughts, neither are your ways my ways, saith the LORD. We grow and develop into seeing situations/circumstances/pain/hurt/rejection/disappointment/devastation/and even hate the way God sees it. …………………………………………...Ephesians 4………………………………………………….. The beauty of the Word can be astonishingly powerful and life changing all at the same-time.

Eph.1:18 - The eyes of your understanding being enlightened; that ye may know what is the hope of his calling, (for you ) and what the riches of the glory of his inheritance in the saints…


We also see the world through God’s perspective in prayer, loving, joy, hope, visions, the Word, church, God’s people and in Obedience.
