The “Plan” of Salvation

The “Plan” of Salvation


A plan - is a detailed proposal for doing or achieving something; an intention or decision about what one is going to do…
A plan gives a detailed description or a map or diagram explaining its purpose
A plan - is to decide on and arrange in advance - to prepare a plan of action

God’s Plan of Salvation has been laid out in His Holy Word. It has been explained in many versions and languages. His plan has existed throughout time; as it will far exceed time. His plan has been made accessible since the beginning of time and made understandable for babes in Christ. His plan as also been placed in elementary form, basic form, simple form, revelation form and human form which is made available all by Faith. This faith is a gift for the soul that has been orchestrated supernaturally for the believing heartmind. If you who have been born into sin this plan is for you. If you think you have not been born into sin then this plan is not for you neither are the benefits of eternal life with Jesus Christ.

Choose ye today whom you will Serve!

Mark 1:15 And saying, the time is fulfilled and the Kingdom of God is at Hand, repent ye and believe the gospel..
Acts 3:19 Repent ye therefore and be converted that your sins may be blotted out when the times of refreshing shall come from the presence of the Lord.

Times of refreshing may come to you when you need to be revived instantly …..
Repent Means - to turn from sin (really what you are turning away from is what you were born into ). Also, He wants you to turn from not knowing Him to Knowing Him, because the sin you were born into blocks Him from your life. He knows It’s not your fault that you were born into sin (but we were born) but, Christ wants you to recognize (who) you are in the sin you were born into. He wants to show you the life he planned for you (as he knew you were going to be born) but, coming to Him you have to (go through) the sin-world you were born into to get to Him, thus by repenting, not for being born but for the causes of affect the world has had on you. Because, no one is born into Righteousness or sinlessness (this comes by the Savior Jesus Christ) at repentance. Which, is God’s Plan for you to repent; to acknowledge that you need Him to clear your sins away. Acknowledging is your accepting and understanding that brings you into the awareness of Jesus Christ.

A Part of the Plan
If a Believer approaches you to evangelize by witnessing to you - it has been arranged by God. If you are sitting in a church service and you are mentally moved - it has been arranged by God. If you are listening to the Word of God in Song - it has been arranged by God. If you read a track it has been arranged by God. If you see a bumper sticker that states, God loves you-it has been arranged by God. If and when you see your bible or a bible - it has been arranged by God. If love has been professed to you that has been arranged by God.
