Learn To Study God's Word!
What does it mean to pursue intimacy with God?


Pursuing Intimacy With God means studying God’s Word - combined with praying, fasting, serving and sharing the wonders of your relationship with God with others - to give strength to your worship and your Christian walk in a way that draws you into the presence of God.
Salvation

Intimacy with God leads to an experience that will, without a doubt, impact and change your life. Let it happen - look forward to, embrace, and expect it. The journey to intimacy with God begins with receiving and understanding the gift of salvation.

For the wages of sin is death; but the gift of God is eternal life through Jesus Christ our Lord.

Romans 6:23
Prayer

Prayer is essential to a strong and personal relationship with God. Prayer is as important as salvation, studying God's Word and fasting - it is your one-on-one with your Savior. It is your time to talk to Him and share everything that is on your heart . . . and for Him to speak to you. Many people are so confused or misinformed about prayer that they don't feel comfortable doing it. Many others who do pray feel they are not doing it "right" and wonder if their prayers have any power or effect. How can we have a fruitful and meaningful prayer life?

And this is the confidence that we have in him, that, if we ask any thing according to his will, he heareth us:

1 John 5:14
Studying God's Word

Study Your Way Into Worship

Studying God’s Word is imperative for a Born-Again Christian. It’s extremely important that we understand that Studying God’s Word is significant as Salvation; you cannot have one without the other. The Word of God shapes/structures your born-again-spiritual- life - by discarding the old you (with the Word) and birthing who (you) were intended to be (by the Word….)
It’s impossible to have an intimate relationship with God and live a victorious life without studying God’s Word daily.
2 Timothy 3:16-17 (complete Jewish Bible Translation) All scripture is God Breathed and is valuable for teaching the truth, convicting of sin, correcting faults and training in right living…

All scripture is given by inspiration of God, and is profitable for doctrine, for reproof, for correction, for instruction in righteousness: That the man of God may be perfect, thoroughly furnished unto all good works.

2 Timothy 3:16-17
Evangelizing

Although Evangelizing is a commandment of God, the commandment becomes what we desire to do. As this great relationship of love grows we become concerned for the things of God, especially building up His Kingdom.
The Great Commission - Mathew 28: 18- 20 And Jesus came and spake unto them, saying, All power is given unto me in heaven and in earth. Go ye therefore, and teach all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost: Teaching them to observe all things whatsoever I have commanded you: and, lo, I am with you always, even unto the end of the world. Amen.
We begin to seek ways and insight from God .... What can I do? We ask this question because of our relationship and love for the LORD.

Then I heard the voice of the LORD saying, "Whom shall I send? And who will go for us? And I said, "Here am I. Send me!"

Isaiah 6:8
