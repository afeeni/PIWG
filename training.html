Preparations



Pray
always be in prayer for lost souls the unsaved…those whom have not come to Christ yetup

Name
(Isa..here am I Lord send me)


Know
That the devil will attack – that he will stir up fear

Fast
To build up your spiritual strength so much (unction – anointing ) comes to help…(He’s always present at conversion)

Study God's Word
Get close to the Holy Spirit by studying and watch the Lord stir up your gifts in you …to do His Will (remember He is gone out before us – prepping hearts to receive Him

Practice
With your family and friends

Rejection
Know that it’s a part of the plan you just do the Will of God; they are not rejecting you but God. They don’t know you to reject you…we cannot take it personal….Jesus Christ has the last Word.

Fear
Is a factor but it is not the end..it will always come ..use it as a barometer as it arise know you are doing the right thing

Reminders & Encouragement
Reminders & Encouragement

  • Plan to go set up days and time and where
  • Pray for the area before you go that day
  • Have a list of churches in the area you Evangelize in
  • Expect – know that when you go – because it’s God’s purpose a soul will be converted TODAY!
  • Partner with someone – (very important ) Mark 6:7
  • T-shirt – create one so when you approach they know why you are coming (they will not run) (someone is expecting you)
  • Be creative – think of ways to witness
  • Opportunities - seek them out
  • Rebuke Judgment – a soul is a soul and a lost one; never let what it looks like be a reason you miss God in an opportunity
  • Approach – without judgment!
