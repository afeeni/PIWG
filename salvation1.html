Salvation Not Born

Salvation not born

When we are born we came into a world of sin prepared for the slaughter of our souls… by the Prince of this world. The sin trap/ambush has no escape except for Salvation, we were born to be REDEEMED by God; He is our only escape. "KJV - Psalms 51:5 Behold I was sharpen in iniquity and in sin did my mother conceive me…"
"NLT- Psalms 51:5 for I was born a sinner, yes, from the moment my mother conceived me… "

Satan’s trap/ambush and influences come to offer us everything in the world to keep us bound/tied to him, to destroy us and to keep us from Salvation… KJV - I John 5:19 - and we know that we are of God, and the whole world lieth in wickedness. John 10:10 - the thief (Satan) cometh not, but for to steal and to kill, and to destroy; I am come (Jesus) that they might have life and have it more abundantly. Jude 1:24-25 now unto him that is able to keep you from falling, and to present you (us) faultless before the presence of his glory with exceeding joy,

To the only wise God our Savior, be glory and majesty, dominion and power, both now and ever. Amen.

Action Taken !

We come into the world knowing how to do wrong we must be taught Righteousness. God sent His Son to die on the Cross for our physical lives and our spiritual lives here on earth… that we may also have eternal life with Him…He knew we could never come to the place of righteousness on our own…. So His Son dies to cover ALL our sins, past, present and future; even with the death/weight of our-sins on him, he never made a commandment to make us choose Him…He allows us to freely come to him by choice…He presents Salvation as a solution/answer for you (us) - John 3:16 - For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life. KJV-For by grace are ye saved through faith; and that not of yourselves: it is the gift of God:


Gift – something given voluntarily without payment…NLT-God saved you by his grace when you believed. And you can't take credit for this; it is a gift from God.

We were born to be bought with the price of Christ Blood (individually) …..His love is the embodiment of the world bestowed to everyone (individually) – we all have access to Him-God. No one religion can House/Retain His Name, His Power, His Authority, even His Word. He made us a diverse people as well as diversity in religion…

7 Go to, let us go down, and there confound (confuse - complicate) their language, that they may not understand one another's speech.
8 So the LORD scattered them abroad from thence upon the face of all the earth: and they left off to build the city.

God’s Word states for You in Matthew 28: 20 - teaching them to observe all things whatsoever I have commanded you. And lo, I am with you always, even unto the end of the world.” Amen.
In the religious/faith that you believe and were baptized in, you are to evangelize the truth that saved and changed you……
There are billions of people waiting to hear what God has done for you – they are looking for Proof and Authentic Salvation. We must become the Word (in) Action they hear about. People are looking for Miracles - Greater works John 14:12 (KJV) 12 Verily, verily, I say unto you, He that believeth on me, the works that I do shall he do also; and greater works than these shall he do; because I go unto my Father because what you believe in your heart will come out; share a testimony, a song, a dance, a spoken word, a preached word, or an encouraged word…..to engage/connect people with the bible for themselves..
