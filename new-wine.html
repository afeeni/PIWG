New Wine

KJV Mark 2:22 - And no man putteth new wine into old bottles: else the new wine doth burst the bottles, and the wine is spilled, and the bottles will be marred - ruined: but new wine must be put into new bottles.

NLT Mark 2:22 - And no one puts new wine into old wine skins. For the wine would burst the wineskins, and the wine and the skins would both be lost. New wine calls for new wine skins.



Wineskin

A skin bottle made of the complete Hide of an animal, such as a goat or a sheep, and used for holding wine. Wine was put into new wineskins, because as it ferments/inflames, it generates carbon dioxide gas that exerts pressure on the skin bottles. New skins expands; old inflexible/stiff ones burst under the pressure.
