BRING JESUS TO ME

Proverbs 4:7 Wisdom is the principal thing; therefore get wisdom: and with all thy getting get understanding...

The Cry of Our Nation

Bring Jesus to Me is the Cry of Our Nation the groanings of a lost and hurting world. A world that’s immersed in the crafting of darkness controlled by satan; whose only freedom is at the hand of the Believer who is willing to obey and Go. This statement is like an insightful revelation waiting to be discovered by a believer. Nevertheless, we have forgotten our day of redemption that we have been set apart to Go. Acts 1:8 states, but ye shall receive power after that the Holy Ghost is come upon you and ye shall be witnesses in Jerusalem, and in all Judaea, and Samaria and unto the uttermost part of the earth. However, we have failed to perceive the meaning of Go. The power is in the understanding to Go, and its wisdom is in what we do and how we go about it.

James 1:5 If any of you lack wisdom, let him ask of God, that giveth to all men liberally, and upbraideth not; and it shall be given him.

Upbraideth means - (without finding fault in you) - nothing you have done or is doing can stop you from asking and receiving wisdom from God. There’s No Judgement!

Proverbs 11:30 KJV - The fruit of the Righteous (you) is a tree of life; and he that winneth souls is wise.

Note: The knowledge you obtain from God is LIFE. You have the opportunity to impart seeds that will grow to produce more people who love Jesus Christ.

Prayer - Father please make the atmosphere conducive for hearing so that understanding my be received and applied to the life of this reader. In Jesus Name I pray, Amen.

The understanding must be captured in our hearts for the love of a Soul; The Cry of a Nation shall be answered. Salvation-Healing will come but don’t let it be said of you...that you did not have time, I don’t know what to do, or that’s not my calling. But, (IF) you are a Believer of Jesus Christ, it’s your calling.

There is a powerful exchange that occurs between two people as death and life breathes the same air simultaneously just for a moment. In order to separate a life from hell into heaven and to bring the kingdom of God to the lost soul.

How ironic for God to place this experience at a converting moment that has been set aside for you the believer to experience. This moment is yours, the living presence of the Lord that shall be with you the rest of your life; evangelizing a soul to life for Jesus Christ.

Statement - usually right after the prayer of Salvation a person is so excited to share coming to christ; as their testimony is most potent to spark a fire in someone else.

The call to heal a nation is within you the Believer, we are the catalyst to bring the Gospel to a sinking world. And, we do it by focusing on Kingdom Building. Bring Jesus to me…!

Prayer: Father here am I send me. Lead me into the path of righteousness for your work sake. Engulf my heart for the love of others that your kingdom may be built; connect me with like minded Believers who are willing to Go. In Jesus Name I ask, Amen.
