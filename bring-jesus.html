BRING JESUS TO ME

Our Nation’s Cry (Exodus 2:24-25)

24 And God heard their groaning, and God remembered his covenant with Abraham, with Isaac, and with Jacob.

25 And God looked upon the children of Israel, and God had respect unto them

Genesis 17:7- And I will establish my covenant between me and thee and thy seed after thee in their generations for an everlasting covenant, to be a God unto thee, and to thy seed after thee.

Acts 2:39- For the promise is unto you, and to your children, and to all that are afar off, even as many as the Lord our God shall call.

Galatians 3:29- And if you are Christ’s, then you are Abraham’s seed, and heirs according to the promise.

Psalm 105:8He remembers His covenant forever, The word which He commanded, for a thousand generations,

Covenant- is a promise is an assurance that one will do a particular thing or a particular thing will happen. (A covenant with God can never be broken)

These words proclaim to us and for us that Jesus Christ (past/present/future) heard our cry before we came to Him. He has prepared the way because He’s that kind of Savior.

This is Christ keeping His Covenant, He established - Ephesians 4:11- 13 which is known for the Five Fold Ministry.

11And he gave some, apostles; and some, prophets; and some, evangelists; and some, pastors and teachers;

12For the perfecting of the saints, for the work of the ministry, for the edifying of the body of Christ:

13Till we all come in the unity of the faith, and of the knowledge of the Son of God, unto a perfect man, unto the measure of the stature of the fulness of Christ:

These scriptures is to bring your awareness to the fact that Christ heard the cry of a nation as He formed a covenant with Abraham that (we the people Abraham’s seed would be taken care of by Jesus Christ)

The message below speaks of the illness that stills the church from a lack of wisdom and understanding of the concept of Go! Which also, is the answer to the groaning of the people; as the promise answer. Christ uses us to heal the groaning of our nation as we come to him...I hear your cry and I am going to send you to be the (go between) to your sisters and brothers (my way is not your way) is the need to Go…

Isaiah 55:8 “For My thoughts are not your thoughts, Nor are your ways My ways,” says the LORD.
