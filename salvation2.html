Knowledge
To come into the knowledge of Jesus Christ to know and understand that you were born a sinner - into a world of sin; it is to know that God the Father LOVED what he created (us) (Psalm 139:14) (Genesis 1:31) but, He also knew that the world we have been born into is under the control of Satan. So He sent His Son Jesus Christ to die on the cross for our sins to separate us from Satan. To distinguish us from other’s for others. But, to receive this BLESSING, we must first acknowledge who Jesus Christ is, admit you are a sinner, ask him to come into your life and understand that He died on the cross for you.

We were born to be saved and given eternal life with the Father - this kind of love is unexplainable.We can know this kind of love - of Christ dying on the cross to save us and give us eternal life - by accepting Jesus Christ as our PERSONAL SAVIOR, the only One who can save us from going to hell. Without SALVATION, we go to hell as punishment for the wrong we continued to do since we were born. We are made right with God by placing our faith in Jesus Christ. And this is true for everyone who believes, no matter who we are.

Who is Satan?

The Bible says that Satan was created by God as a cherub, the most powerful of God's angelic beings. Sometime after his creation and before the creation of mankind, Satan rebelled against God and took one third of the angels (now referred to as demons) with him into rebellion (into the earth) Satan’s rebellion became his character – telling people lies, stirring up hatred, sexual immorality, impurity, debauchery, idolatry, witchcraft, discord, jealousy, fits of rage, selfish ambition, envy, drunkenness, orgies, enslavement to sin Job 2:2 And the LORD said to Satan, "Where have you come from?" Satan answered the LORD, "From roaming throughout the earth, going back and forth on it." Where did he come from? Satan came from God in Heaven – he was one of God’s angels in the position of great authority …. Why is he evil? Because of Rebellion - revolting against righteousness, authority, turns away from the right thing to do which is the normal thing to do…not in its nature to do so, disgusted with love, obeying, and seeks harm not peace and enjoys our displeasures… causes disasters in families, communities, cities, and in love, in children, and even in the world that he’s prince of… Rebellion – is the character of Satan, it binds you to the untruth and it forms a hardened heart, and keeps you from even wanting to know anything about truth. It only seeks to please what is wrong/negative/hateful/unhappy/disrespectful/depressing and everything that can opposes Jesus Christ. It loves pleasing the flesh and not the spirit, and as long as he can fill you with rebellious thoughts and acts of satisfying self….he wins every time…this is why it is important to hear the Word of God - it’s the ONLY thing in earth that can deliver us from the enemy…that can keep us out of Satan’s dwelling place, bring us out of darkness…Luke 11:28 Blessed are those that hear the Word and obey it… Why does he have the power he has? God is not a God who should lie or will lie. He gave him the authority and power in the position that he was in - in heaven as an angel… the same free will we have they have also.. Too choose love or rebellion….Satan choose rebellion… so he was removed without having his power revoked as he was kicked out of heaven into the earth….
