Hardened Not Your Heart

Hardened not your Heart - Hebrews 3:7-8 read (3) translations

Harden not your heart after your time of testing/need, sorrow, heartbreak, loneliness, rejection, or despair; our relationship with Jesus Christ does not exempt us from life’s troubles. The key is not letting the devil (snatch) the joy of Christ from comforting you and answering your prayers. It’s that moment when hope-faith and understanding comes together that reveals a (revelation) of an assurance that God is with us...expect and wait for it. Isaiah 41:10 Fear thou not; for I am with thee: be not dismayed; for I am thy God: I will strengthen thee; yea, I will help thee; yea, I will uphold thee with the right hand of my righteousness.

Never Draw Back Always Run to Him - (no matter where you have fallen short) it’s not about if you have sinned, backslidden, lack of church or no church; Jesus Christ will strengthen you…

Matthew 11:28 Come unto me, all ye that labour and are heavy laden, and I will give you rest.

God has made promises to us that can be found in your bible between Genesis – Revelations. He’s not a God that goes to the cross for our sins and then leave you when trouble, trials and tribulations comes your way…

Surviving/living life outside of the Word of God is possible, it’s done every day by the unsaved and the saved (Matthew 5:45).. But, to have the knowledge of God during a crisis; and you know what His promises are for you: Now this is the experience of peace that passes all understanding, peace in the midst of a storm; this is why we never draw back and not develop a hardened heart….

Actually, our troubles are our boot camp exercises preparing us for battle to fight for the Kingdom - meaning you’re calling and ministry. Trails don’t come to destroy us they come for character building. They produce tools and weapons called, prayer, fasting, trusting, believing, discernment, vision, creativity, understanding and wisdom. All key elements for working in the Kingdom.

Every Believer has a calling - to the Kingdom of God; you have been set aside ___________finish

Anyone that glorifies God will be attacked…but, we have learned to fight based on what we have already been through… we have proof that we win because we are the results. We’ve seen so many answered prayers, and so many victories enough to know that God’s work will be accomplished, visions will be manifested and purpose fulfilled and Souls Saved…..

Because we have learned - No Weapon Formed Against me/us shall prosper ….

Never Draw Back!
